Biography

The SIDH are a young and energetic band, always looking for new ideas and musical arrangements, without forgetting their roots. Original and innovative, The result is a combination of highly contemporary and modern Celtic music, made of Whistles and Bagpipes, Keyboards and Percussions, Hip Hop and Breton beats, able to recreate, according to Hevia, atmospheres with a distinctive scent of “future tradition”.

Despite their young age, the SIDH can boast of collaborations with internationally renown artists such as Carlos Nuñez, Hevia, Paddy Moloney (Chieftains), Lindsey Stirling, Flook, Barrage, Athy, Dealan, Dominic Graham School of Irish Dance, Red Hot Chilli Pipers, etc.
